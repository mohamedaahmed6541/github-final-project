## Simple Interest Calculator
A shell script that calculates simple interest given principal, annual rate of interest, and time period in years.

### Formula:
The formula for simple interest is:
**I = P * R * T**

Where:
* `P` = Principal amount
* `R` = Annual interest rate
* `T` = Time period in years

### Input:
* `p`: principal amount
* `t`: time period in years
* `r`: annual rate of interest

### Output:
* `Simple Interest`: The calculated interest amount based on the inputs provided.

### Example:
- **Input**: Principal = 1000, Rate = 5%, Time = 2 years
- **Output**: Simple Interest = 100
